What is Midnight Tee?

A live stage for music that doesn't need a label to exist.

"AI gave people who never had access a way to create. Midnight Tee gives them somewhere to be heard."

Most AI music sits in a folder or gets lost in a Reddit thread. Midnight Tee is the first place it gets played out loud — in front of an audience that came specifically to discover what you made. No gatekeeping. No pretension. Just honest energy and a room full of people who actually care.

How it works

Simple queue. Real crowd.
No mystery.

01
Submit your track

Drop your song into the queue before or during the stream. AI-generated music only — Suno, Riffusion, Udio, whatever you're working with.

02
Show up when it plays

Artists must be present in chat when their track drops. This isn't a dropbox — it's a room. Be there for your moment.

03
The room reacts

Chat responds in real time. Tee gives honest feedback if you want it. The vibe is supportive. The bar is real.

04
Boost your spot

Priority slots let you move up the queue. Use them or don't — either way, the queue keeps moving and your track gets heard.

Community & culture

For the ones who were always told they needed more.

More money. More equipment. More training. More connections. AI music changed that overnight — and most of the world still hasn't caught up to what that means.

Midnight Tee caught up. A community built around the belief that the person who made a beat at 2am on a free tool deserves the same stage as anyone else. The music is the credential. Nothing else.

We're not pretending this is perfect. AI music is still finding itself — and so are the people making it. That's exactly why this space exists. To figure it out together, in public, with good energy.
